PCOSSignal Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

PCOSSignal is designed to help women with PCOS track their health data locally on their device. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

  • All data is stored locally on your device. PCOSSignal uses Apple SwiftData to store your cycles, symptoms, medications, and lab results directly on your iPhone or iPad.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal health data to any external servers.
  • iCloud Sync (optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account protected by your Apple ID. We do not have access to this data.

Health Data

  • PCOSSignal handles sensitive health information. All data remains on your device and in your personal iCloud vault if sync is enabled.
  • We do not sell or share your health data with third parties, advertisers, or insurance companies.

Health Disclaimer

PCOSSignal is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Cycle phase estimates, pattern alerts, and correlations are informational only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Notifications

PCOSSignal may send local notifications to remind you to log medications or symptoms. You can disable these at any time in app settings or iOS Notification Settings.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
