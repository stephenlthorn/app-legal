Privacy Policy for FlowCast Finance

Effective Date: February 26, 2026
Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Introduction

FlowCast Finance ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Contact: stephenlthorn@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Financial data you manually enter (transactions, invoices, income, expenses)
  • Tax profile information (filing status, estimated income, state of residence)
  • Account information (email address for account creation)
  • CSV imports (transaction data you choose to upload)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (app features used, session duration, crash reports)
  • Push notification tokens (to send tax reminders and alerts)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not connect to your bank accounts
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or location
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (calculate "safe to spend," track invoices, estimate taxes)
  • Send notifications (quarterly tax reminders, invoice due date alerts)
  • Improve the app (analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, optimize performance)
  • Provide customer support (respond to your inquiries)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Cloud storage: Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) — encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Local storage: Some data cached on your device for offline access

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Firebase security rules restrict access to your data (only you can access it)
  • We do not store bank credentials (manual entry only)

3.3 Data Retention

  • Your data is stored as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account anytime via Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Upon deletion, all data is permanently removed within 30 days

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Firebase (Google) Cloud database, authentication, push notifications Firebase Privacy
Apple Push Notification Service Push notifications for iOS Apple Privacy

These services may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — export all data via Settings → Privacy → Export Data
  • Delete your data — permanently delete your account via Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Opt out of notifications — disable push notifications in iOS Settings or app Settings
  • Request data correction — contact us at stephenlthorn@gmail.com

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to data portability (export your data in JSON format)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing

CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)

To exercise these rights, email stephenlthorn@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

FlowCast Finance is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at stephenlthorn@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Tax Calculation Disclaimer

Important: FlowCast Finance provides tax estimates for informational purposes only. We are not tax professionals and do not provide tax advice. Tax calculations may be inaccurate. Always consult a CPA or tax advisor before making tax payments or financial decisions.
