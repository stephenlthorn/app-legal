2. Description of Service

HAM Signal is a mobile app for amateur radio operators that displays real-time solar and space weather data sourced from public NOAA feeds, including:

  • Solar Flux Index, K-Index, A-Index, sunspot count, and X-Ray classification
  • HF band propagation forecasts for 80m through 10m (day and night)
  • Solar disk imagery from NASA's SDO satellite
  • Day/night terminator (greyline) maps
  • Custom alerts for solar flares, geomagnetic storms, and other space weather events
  • Home screen and lock screen widgets

Important: HAM Signal is an informational tool only. It does not replace official aviation, maritime, or emergency communications guidance.
