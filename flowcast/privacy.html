Privacy Policy for FlowCast Finance

Effective Date: February 26, 2026
Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Introduction

FlowCast Finance ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Contact: stephenlthorn@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Financial data you manually enter (transactions, invoices, income, expenses)
  • Tax profile information (filing status, estimated income, state of residence)
  • Account information (email address for account creation, if applicable)
  • CSV imports (transaction data you choose to upload)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage data (app features used, crash reports for debugging)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not connect to your bank accounts
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or location
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (calculate "safe to spend," track invoices, estimate taxes)
  • Send notifications (quarterly tax reminders, invoice due date alerts, if enabled)
  • Improve the app (analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, optimize performance)
  • Provide customer support (respond to your inquiries)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Local storage: All financial data is stored locally on your device
  • iCloud backup: If enabled, data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account (controlled by you)

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data is stored securely on your device using iOS security features
  • We do not transmit your financial data to external servers
  • We do not store bank credentials (manual entry only)

3.3 Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete the app
  • You can clear all data anytime via Settings → Delete All Data

4. Third-Party Services

FlowCast Finance uses minimal third-party services:

  • Apple Push Notification Service (optional): To send tax reminders if you enable notifications. See Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple Analytics (optional): If you allow analytics in iOS settings, anonymous usage data may be collected by Apple

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all data is stored locally on your device
  • Export your data — use Settings → Export Data to save a CSV/JSON file
  • Delete your data — use Settings → Delete All Data or uninstall the app
  • Opt out of notifications — disable in iOS Settings or app Settings

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to data portability (export your data)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten" — delete all data)
  • Right to restrict processing

CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect (see Section 1)
  • Request deletion of your personal information (delete the app or clear data)
  • Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)

To exercise these rights, email stephenlthorn@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

FlowCast Finance is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at stephenlthorn@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Tax Calculation Disclaimer

Important: FlowCast Finance provides tax estimates for informational purposes only. We are not tax professionals and do not provide tax advice. Tax calculations may be inaccurate. Always consult a CPA or tax advisor before making tax payments or financial decisions.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy?
